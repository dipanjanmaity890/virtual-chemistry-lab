Greenhouse Effect & Global Warming

Earth absorbs solar radiation, heats up, and re-radiates heat as infrared radiation. Greenhouse gases trap this heat, maintaining habitable temperature.

Major Greenhouse Gases: • Carbon Dioxide ($CO_2$): primary feedback driver. • Methane ($CH_4$): 25x more potent heat trap than $CO_2$. • Nitrous Oxide ($N_2O$), Water Vapor, and CFCs.

Acid Rain

Rainwater pH drops below 5.6 due to reactions of oxides of sulfur and nitrogen in atmosphere with water vapor:

$$2SO_2 + O_2 + 2H_2O \rightarrow 2H_2SO_4$$

$$4NO_2 + O_2 + 2H_2O \rightarrow 4HNO_3$$

Damage: Corrodes marble structures (e.g. Taj Mahal: $CaCO_3 + H_2SO_4 \rightarrow CaSO_4 + H_2O + CO_2$) and damages agricultural soils.
