Class 9 Practical

Solutions & Tyndall Effect

Prepare true solutions, suspensions, and colloids. Distinguish them by testing light scattering (Tyndall Effect) and filtration.

Class 9 Practical

Mixture vs Compound

Differentiate between an iron-sulphur mixture and iron sulphide compound by checking magnetic behavior and reaction with CS₂.

Class 9 Practical

Physical vs Chemical Changes

Observe the burning of magnesium ribbon, reaction of zinc with acid, and transitions of ice and water to identify changes.

Class 10 Practical

pH Strip Determination

Determine the pH of hydrochloric acid, lemon juice, water, baking soda, and sodium hydroxide using universal indicator test strips.

Class 10 Practical

Types of Chemical Reactions

Simulate combination of lime and water, thermal decomposition of ferrous sulphate, and double displacement reactions.

Class 10 & 11 Practical

Flame Tests & Salt Analysis

Identify cations (Calcium, Barium, Potassium, Sodium, Copper) using Bunsen flame colors, and verify acidic radicals (carbonates, chlorides, sulfates) through wet tests.

Class 11 Practical

Glass Working & Lab Techniques

Learn introductory glass manipulations including cutting/bending glass tubing and boring rubber cork stopper holes.

Class 11 Practical

Crystallisation & MP/BP

Purify impure samples of Copper Sulfate or Benzoic Acid by hot filtration, recrystallization, and determining melting/boiling points.

Class 11 Practical

Chemical Equilibrium Shift

Study the reversible equilibrium between Ferric ions and Thiocyanate. Shift the equilibrium using concentration changes (Le Chatelier).

Class 12 Practical

Volumetric Titration Lab

Determine the strength of a given Sodium Hydroxide (NaOH) solution by titrating it against standard 0.1M Oxalic Acid using phenolphthalein.

Class 12 Practical

Organic Functional Groups Lab

Perform qualitative chemical tests for organic compounds: Alcohols (Lucas), Phenols (FeCl₃), Aldehydes (Tollens'), Ketones (2,4-DNP), Acids (NaHCO₃), and Amines (Carbylamine/Hinsberg).

Class 12 Practical

Double Salt Preparation

Synthesize pure crystals of Potash Alum [K₂SO₄·Al₂(SO₄)₃·24H₂O] or Mohr's Salt in an inorganic crystallisation process.
