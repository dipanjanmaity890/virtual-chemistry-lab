s-Block Elements

Group 1 (Alkali Metals) & Group 2 (Alkaline Earth Metals) properties, diagonal relationships, and compounds.

Characteristics of Alkali Metals

  • Valence shell configuration: $ns^1$. Highly electropositive.
  • Low ionization enthalpies, decreasing down the group as size increases.
  • Flame colors: Cations are excited in burner flames and emit characteristic wavelengths:
    • Lithium: **Crimson** | Sodium: **Yellow** | Potassium: **Lilac**

Solvay Process

Industrial manufacture of Sodium Carbonate ($Na_2CO_3$):

$$2NH_3 + CO_2 + H_2O \rightarrow (NH_4)_2CO_3$$

$$(NH_4)_2CO_3 + CO_2 + H_2O \rightarrow 2NH_4HCO_3$$

$$NH_4HCO_3 + NaCl \rightarrow NH_4Cl + NaHCO_3 \downarrow$$

Heating Sodium Bicarbonate yields soda ash: $$2NaHCO_3 \rightarrow Na_2CO_3 + CO_2 + H_2O$$
