p-Block Elements (Group 13 & 14)

Boron family, carbon allotropes, catenation, silicates, and Borax bead assays.

Group 13: Boron Family

  • Valence shell configuration: $ns^2 np^1$.
  • Boron is non-metal, forms electron-deficient compounds ($BF_3$, $B_2H_6$).
  • Diboran ($B_2H_6$) bridge structure: Includes 4 terminal 2-centre-2-electron $B-H$ bonds, and 2 bridge 3-centre-2-electron "banana" bonds.

Group 14: Carbon Family

  • Valence shell configuration: $ns^2 np^2$. Shows strong catenation capacity.
  • Allotropes of Carbon:
    • **Diamond**: $sp^3$ covalent network (extremely hard).
    • **Graphite**: $sp^2$ layered structure with free pi electrons (lubricant/conductor).
    • **Fullerenes ($C_{60}$)**: cage structures.
